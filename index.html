The Best BLT Recipe Ever !!!

Learn how to make a yummy BLT sandwich in less than ten minutes!

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Tomato
  • Mayonnaise
  • Bacon
  • Lettuce

Instructions:

  1. Lay out two slices of bread (toast bread if wanted)
  2. Squirt mayonnaise over one slice
  3. Cook bacon until done
  4. Put bacon, lettuce, and tomato on the other slice
  5. Put the two slices together
  6. Enjoy !
